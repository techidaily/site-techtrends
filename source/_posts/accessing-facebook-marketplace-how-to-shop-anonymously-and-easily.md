##  Do You Need an Account to Use Facebook Marketplace?

 You can browse and search Facebook Marketplace without signing in, but you need an account to communicate with sellers or to sell your own stuff.

![Sign in to Facebook Marketplace.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/2023-04-20_16-55-53.png) 

 Unless the seller has an alternative contact method listed in the description of the item, Facebook Messenger is the only way to communicate with them. [You don't need an active Facebook account to use Facebook Messenger](https://instagram-video-recordings.techidaily.com/in-2024-the-quick-guide-to-instagram-video-speed-up-mobiledesktop/), but you're still required to be signed into Facebook proper to initiate a conversation with a seller.

 You'd think requiring Facebook accounts would cut down on scams, but there is no shortage of [Facebook Marketplace scams to watch out for](https://fox-access.techidaily.com/updated-transform-overload-into-order-with-skillful-tiktok-saves-edits-for-2024/). Craigslist and eBay still exist as well, but many people have moved to Facebook Marketplace for selling and buying used goods locally. You'll have to decide which one is right for you.
