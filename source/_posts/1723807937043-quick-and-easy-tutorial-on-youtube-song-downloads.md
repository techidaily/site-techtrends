 This is the simplest way to download music from YouTube. You don’t need to download third-party program. And you don’t need to pay for the download. Many websites out there provide the service allowing you to download music from YouTube. Be careful when you choose the website, as some websites may trick you to go to the advertisement page before downloading.

 1)**Copy and paste your YouTube video URL** to the box and click**START** .
