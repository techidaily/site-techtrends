## Complete Walkthrough: How to Enable Cookies in Any Internet Browser
### What to Know

* Every browser works a little differently; instructions can also vary a bit across operating systems.
* In most cases, open the browser's settings and search for**cookies** or browse the privacy options.
* Web browsers typically let you enable all cookies or pick specific sites to turn on cookies there only.

 This article explains how to enable cookies in Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. This works very similarly in other browsers.

## How to Enable Chrome Cookies

 Switching cookies on for Chrome works the same way in the Android app and the desktop app. iOS users don't need to worry about this because Chrome automatically enables cookies for you. (You can't disable them so don't bother trying.)

1. Select the**three dots** at the top right and choose**Settings** from the list of options.  
![The Google Chrome menu](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/YNTvQs67-E-n8RGLcv8w_WbaMzE=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/chrome-menu-e25bc7f3057e4381a6c369b1595fe8bb.png)
2. Go to**Privacy and security** \>**Third-party cookies** .  
![The Privacy and Security settings in Chrome](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/KB4LdF4rZeQOMhg3ACVsfUSf7tI=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/chrome-privacy-and-security-settings-1517a044606041eab12b5778cb63e120.png)
3. Select**Allow third-party cookies** .

## How to Enable Cookies in Firefox

 Firefox lets you control cookies via its[Enhanced Tracking Protection feature](https://www.lifewire.com/what-is-firefox-quantum-4177074) . Here's how it works in the desktop and mobile apps:

1. Select the**menu** at the top right of the browser, then choose**Settings** from the list.  
![The Firefox menu](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/j2XtZzvZxgpDSbxO6jR8moRd5RU=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/firefox-menu-46c6b942e71a4406ac51133c69d2c324.png)
2. Select**Privacy & Security** on the left if you're on a computer, or tap**Enhanced Tracking Protection** in the app.
3. Select**Standard** to allow cookies. Or, choose**Custom** but make sure**Cookies** is unchecked.  
![The Standard tracking protection option chosen in Firefox settings](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/AJ-ikcc0K9EYYeUEQ0wDWqMpPKg=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/firefox-privacy-settings-144ec2396a084d22a98e4ac0cdaef536.png)

## How to Enable Cookies in Edge

 These directions explain how to allow cookies for all sites or specific sites in the Microsoft Edge desktop software:

1. Click the**three dots** at the top right of the program to open the main menu, then choose**Settings** .  
![All the menu options in the Edge browser for Windows 11](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/_8Z-ohf3_dzQ04WxAHIXu7ogIrI=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/edge-settings-menu-55f164e708ab4f3883a9215bbaf421e2.png)
2. Choose**Cookies and site permissions** from the left pane.
3. Select**Manage and delete cookies and site data** on the right.  
![Microsoft Edge's settings for cookies and site permissions](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/hf2HP51uHxfru1DXfC6Os5M9Cds=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/microsoft-edge-cookies-site-permissions-30a7d623c4554bab9f49567bb30484e6.png)
4. Tap the toggle for **Allow sites to save and read cookie data (recommended)** to switch it on.

## How to Enable Cookies in Safari

 The option to enable Safari cookies is in the browser's advanced settings. Here's how to get there in the mobile app and then in the desktop app:

### Safari Mobile App

 The[default iOS web browser](https://www.lifewire.com/using-safari-iphone-browser-2000784) has cookies enabled by default. Follow these steps to check the setting on your iPhone or iPad:

1. Open the**Settings** app.
2. Select**Safari** \>**Advanced** .
3. Turn off the**Block All Cookies** toggle.  
![The Block All Cookies toggle switched off in Safari's settings for iPhone](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/f7Mnboot_z9KfOUCoFKp-QskaoI=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/iphone-advanced-safari-settings-fbf4eb72707347eab0e2aefd244b7159.png)
