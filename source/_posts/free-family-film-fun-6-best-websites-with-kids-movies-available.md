 What We Like

* Larger collection than other sites.
* No sign-up required.
* Pulls from other streaming services.

 What We Don't Like

* A lot of movies listed are not free to watch.

 Yidio searches all the free movie websites and puts lists together so that all the movies are in one place, including free movies for kids. To view these titles, select**Genre** \>**Kids and Family** .

 Inside this movie section, you can sort by popularity and recently added selections, and filter the kids movies by rating such as G, PG, and PG-13.

 Some great free kids movies we've spotted here include _Escape From Planet Earth, Hoodwinked Too!, Spirit of the Forest, Cop Dog, The Snowman, Mia and the Migoo, Finding Rin Tin Tin, Lion of Oz_ , and_Soccer Mom._

 What We Like

* "Only Free on Tubi" section has site-exclusive titles.

 What We Don't Like

 Tubi has a section for family movies and kids' shows, and even an entirely separate area of the site called Tubi Kids. Although you can't organize the films by rating or popularity, a single, full page of free kids' movies makes it easy to browse.

 _The Happy Elf, All Dogs Go to Heaven, Igor, The Secret of Nimh, Little Bear Movie, The Amazing Feats of Young Hercules, Eloise: Eloise Goes to School,_ and_Cirque Du Soleil: Worlds Away_ are just some of the movies we've seen available at Tubi.

 What We Like

* No user account needed.
* All the kids titles are on one page.

 What We Don't Like

* Relatively few titles.
* Movies are mixed with TV shows.
