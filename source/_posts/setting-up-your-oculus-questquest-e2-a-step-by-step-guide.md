###  What to Know

* You can connect and disconnect a Meta account from Facebook at any time in the**Accounts Center** .

##  How to Connect Your Quest to Your New Meta Account

 After you’ve successfully created your Meta account, you’ll need to connect your Quest to your new account. Make this link via the Meta website on either PC or mobile. Here’s how to finish the process:  

1. Put on your headset, and make note of the code.
2. Navigate to[ Meta's device page](https://auth.meta.com/device) , enter your code, and select**Connect your device** .

##  How to Separate a Meta Account From Facebook

 If you owned a Quest prior to the introduction of Meta accounts, then your Facebook account is tied to any[ games you bought on Quest](https://www.lifewire.com/buy-games-on-meta-oculus-quest-2-6362981) , your VR profile, your headset, and the Meta Quest[ app](https://www.lifewire.com/what-are-apps-1616114) .

 You can leave it that way if you want, or you can separate your Meta account so that the two are no longer linked. The Meta account will inherit all of your Quest purchases, and from that point on you'll use it to log in to your Quest.

 You can connect and disconnect Meta accounts from Facebook accounts freely the same way you can link and[ unlink Instagram from Facebook](https://www.lifewire.com/unlink-facebook-from-instagram-4428078) .

 Here’s how to disconnect your Meta account from your Facebook account:  

1. Navigate to[ Meta's account center's profile page](https://accountscenter.meta.com/) , and select**Accounts** .  
![Accounts highlighted on Facebook Accounts Center.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/PgruU256W2B1U0mb2Q97A33a5Lw=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/008_how-to-create-a-meta-oculus-quest-or-quest-2-account-6503595-0d2ae5ac76744726a61291fdcca43aec.jpg)
2. Locate your Meta account and select**Remove** .  
![Remove highlighted on a Meta account card on Accounts Center.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/7ufXSaEJMHqJten-Ou_lM62XmW8=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/009_how-to-create-a-meta-oculus-quest-or-quest-2-account-6503595-d8b9872be2de4121b66e3ff65f00fce0.jpg)
3. Select**Continue** .  

![Continue highlighted on Accounts Center.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/xlj8yZM-VqVLV1CzFwZrdKGesnE=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/remove3-4f062fd8e67643c09c41517f2c9efe73.jpg)

![Remove (name) highlighted on Accounts Center.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/N4w3eL9MaOtAY-mUpQx2H5A9eG8=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/010_how-to-create-a-meta-oculus-quest-or-quest-2-account-6503595-f71157308d6d4d249eed864d07fadea9.jpg)

##  How Do You Make a Meta Account?

 You can make a Meta account using an email address, with a[ Facebook](https://www.lifewire.com/what-is-facebook-3486391) or[ Instagram](https://www.lifewire.com/what-is-instagram-3486316) account, or by separating your existing Quest profile from your Facebook account. Here are a few details to keep in mind:

* **Setup methods** : Account setup happens through the Meta website on both PC and mobile if you’re making a new account.
* **Existing Facebook users** : Users who have previously set up their Quest using Facebook and want to keep games they’ve purchased need to create a Meta account using their Facebook account. If you use this method, you’ll have the choice to create an entirely separate Meta account that retains all of your previous game purchases, or create a Meta account that’s tied to your Facebook account.

 Those who select the latter option will still be able to separate their Meta and Facebook accounts in the future via the Accounts Center.

* **New VR users** : Users who have never used[ virtual reality](https://www.lifewire.com/virtual-reality-vr-definition-4155090) (VR) before and don’t want to associate their Meta account with a Facebook account can create a brand-new account on the Meta website using just an email address. This same procedure also works for anyone who has previously used their Quest but doesn't want to keep using an existing VR profile.

 If you’ve just bought a Quest and haven’t used it yet, the process of[ setting up your Quest](https://www.lifewire.com/set-up-meta-oculus-quest-2-6363039) will create your Meta account. Put the headset on, write down the code you see there, and enter the code into the Meta Quest app on your phone. You'll have the option to sign in with Facebook, Instagram, or an email address to create your Meta account.

 FAQ

* How do I switch admin accounts on my Oculus Quest 2?  
 The only way to change the admin account is to[ reset your Quest 2](https://www.lifewire.com/factory-reset-oculus-quest-or-oculus-quest-2-5113075) to factory settings. Then, set up your system with the other account.
* Can my Quest 2 have multiple accounts?  
 Yes. You can add up to three additional accounts to an Oculus Quest 2\. Use the original admin account to[ set up multiple accounts on your Quest 2](https://www.lifewire.com/add-multiple-accounts-to-oculus-quest-2-5115935) .
* How do I delete an account on my Oculus Quest 2?  
 To remove an account, go to**Settings** \>**Accounts Center** \>**Accounts** and choose**Remove** next to the account. However, you cannot remove the admin account.
