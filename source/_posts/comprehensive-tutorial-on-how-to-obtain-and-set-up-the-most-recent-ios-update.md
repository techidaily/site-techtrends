### What to Know

* Connect iPhone to computer > Open**iTunes** \>**Sync** or**Back Up Now** \>**Check for Update** \>**Update** .
* Choose**Download and Install** or**Download Only** .
* Click**Next** \>**Agree** to start the download and installation process.

 This article explains how to download and install new iOS updates using iTunes on a Mac or PC. It applies to iTunes 11 and later and Macs running macOS Mojave (10.14) and earlier and PCs running Windows 10 and later.

 When Apple releases[a new update to iOS](https://www.lifewire.com/ios-versions-4147730) —the operating system that runs the[iPhone](https://www.lifewire.com/pick-best-iphone-for-you-1999293) —install it. Upgrades to iOS deliver bug fixes, interface changes, and new features.

## How to Update iOS Using iTunes

 If your Mac or PC has iTunes, you can upgrade to the latest version of iOS through the program. Using iTunes to update your iPhone or[iPad](https://www.lifewire.com/why-cant-i-upgrade-ipad-1994326) is a good option if your iOS device is low on space.

 Alternatively, you can[update your iOS wirelessly](https://www.lifewire.com/upgrade-ios-on-iphone-connecting-itunes-1999188) directly from your iPhone.

 Here's how to perform an iOS update using iTunes on[macOS](https://www.lifewire.com/what-is-macos-4691239) and[Windows](https://www.lifewire.com/brief-history-of-microsoft-windows-3507078) computers.

1. Connect the iOS device to the computer you sync it with, then open iTunes.
2. Select the icon for the device to open the device management screen.  
![How to restore iPhone](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/64V3QGUEgeSdmkWN46eKp1DACBY=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/001-how-to-restore-an-iphone-577630-96c8daae9421440fb79e6b2625de9d57.jpg)
3. Select**Sync** to[sync the device with the computer](https://www.lifewire.com/sync-iphone-to-computer-2000128) . Or, select**Back Up Now** to create a backup of the data on the phone. It's good to have a backup[in case anything goes wrong](https://www.lifewire.com/restore-iphone-from-backup-2000296) with the upgrade.

6. Read the information about new features, fixes, and changes the new version of the iOS offers, then select**Next** .
7. Select**Agree** to acknowledge the user agreement.
8. The update downloads and automatically installs on your device. If prompted, follow the instructions.
9. When the installation is complete, the device will automatically restart.

 A warning appears if there isn't enough free storage space on the device to install the update. Delete apps and[free up space on your iPhone](https://www.lifewire.com/update-iphone-with-low-storage-4125574) to solve the problem.

## Finish the Upgrade on the iPhone

 To complete an iOS update, accept the licensing terms, review information about the update's new features, and configure new settings.

 Follow the onscreen prompts, and in a few taps, you'll be using your newly upgraded device.
