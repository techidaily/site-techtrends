### Compatibility & Requirements

**MacOS:**

* Make sure your WebEx Meetings app is up-to-date. You can download the latest update [here](https://www.webex.com/downloads.html).
* Make sure Virtual Webcams are [enabled within the WebEx app](https://help.webex.com/en-us/yartze/Enable-or-Disable-Virtual-Cameras-in-Cisco-Webex-Meetings).

**Windows:**

Make sure your WebEx Meetings app is up-to-date. You can download the latest update [here](https://www.webex.com/downloads.html).
