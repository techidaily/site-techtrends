##  How to Use Your Twitch Account With Two Factor Authentication 

 Once you've set 2FA on Twitch, you'll need to log in slightly differently than before. Here's what to expect:

1. Log in to Twitch as you would normally.
2. Open the authenticator app you used to set up two-factor authentication, find**Twitch** in the list, and note the 2FA code next to it.  
 If you can't find the app or you no longer have access to it, select the**here** link below the text box to authenticate via SMS.
3. Enter the 2FA code into the**Token** box in your browser, then click**Submit** to log in.
