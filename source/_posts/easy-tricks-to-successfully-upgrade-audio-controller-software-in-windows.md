##  Use Device Manager to Update Sound Drivers

 The easiest way to update your audio drivers is to [use Windows' built-in Device Manager tool](https://facebook-video-content.techidaily.com/updated-unleashing-your-creative-potential-in-making-fb-reels-for-2024/). This tool automatically finds and installs the latest drivers for your devices, including your sound card drivers.

 To use it, open the "Start" menu, search for "Device Manager", and select the tool.

![Select &quot;Device Manager.&quot;](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/1-open-windows-device-manager.png) 

 In Device Manager, beside "Sound, Video and Game Controllers," click the right-arrow icon.

![Click the right-arrow icon beside "Sound, Video and Game Controllers."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/2-device-manager-audio-devices.png) 

 In the expanded menu, [right-click](https://desktop-recording.techidaily.com/updated-the-art-of-recording-fun-6-techniques-to-document-minecraft-for-2024/) your audio card and choose "Update Driver."

![Choose "Update Driver."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/3-device-manager-update-audio-drivers.png)

##  Use Windows Update to Get the Latest Audio Drivers

 Another way to update your PC's various drivers is to use Windows Update. This feature [checks for Windows operating system updates](https://extra-tips.techidaily.com/innovative-ai-tools-to-spark-your-podcast-written-name/) and for updates to your attached hardware devices, including your audio card.

 It's worth trying this method if Device Manager couldn't find the latest drivers for your sound card.

[Start the driver update process](https://bypass-frp.techidaily.com/is-gsm-flasher-adb-legit-full-review-to-bypass-your-xiaomi-redmi-13c-5g-phone-frp-lock-by-drfone-android/) by launching Settings using Windows+i. Then, select "Update & Security."

![Choose "Update & Security" in Settings.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/5-windows-10-update-security.png) 

 In the sidebar on the left, click "Windows Update." On the right pane, select "Check for Updates."

![Select Windows Update > Check for Updates.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/6-update-windows-10.png) 

 Install the available updates, and your audio drivers will be updated simultaneously _if_ there is a driver update available. Then, [reboot your Windows 10 PC](https://article-posts.techidaily.com/comparing-the-creme-de-la-creme-gopro-hero5-black-to-hero4-silver-for-2024/).
