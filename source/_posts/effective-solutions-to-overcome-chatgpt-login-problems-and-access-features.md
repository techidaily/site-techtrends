The first thing to do if you're having trouble logging in is to make sure that your username and password are correct.

 If OpenAI's servers are encountering issues, the only thing you can do is come back at a later time and try again. If you can't even reach the login screen or you get a "ChatGPT is at capacity right now" error then this suggests the issue is with the server rather than your setup. You can always take a peek at the [OpenAI status page](https://status.openai.com/) to confirm.
