## Unlocking Google's Gemini Potential: Comprehensive Instructions for Activating Gemini AI on Android Phones

### Quick Links

* [How to Install Google Gemini AI on Android](https://fox-blue.techidaily.com/mastering-the-name-game-for-top-podcast-titles/)

### Key Takeaways

* Gemini replaces Google Assistant on Android devices once installed and cannot be used separately.
* It is possible to switch back to Google Assistant from Gemini by changing the default digital assistant app in settings.

 Google Bard was replaced by Gemini, the company's latest and greatest AI tool, and it's much more than just a chatbot. Eventually, Gemini will replace Google Assistant on Android, but you don't have to wait to try it for yourself right now. We'll show you how to get started.

 Gemini is not simply a [rebranded version of Bard](https://location-social.techidaily.com/does-find-my-friends-work-on-vivo-x90s-drfone-by-drfone-virtual-android/). It's actually a combination of Bard and Duet AI, now known as "Gemini Pro." Not only can Gemini do text-based tasks that reportedly outperform [GPT-4](https://extra-support.techidaily.com/new-pictorial-perfection-proven-techniques-for-stunning-photos/), but it also has image generation to rival [DALL-E](https://extra-guidance.techidaily.com/step-by-step-process-for-gradual-audio-fading-in-premiere-for-2024/). If you're willing to shell out $20 per month, you can get access to [Gemini Advanced](https://gemini.google.com/advanced?utm%5Fsource=gemini&utm%5Fmedium=web&utm%5Fcampaign=gemini%5Fadvanced%5Fannounce%5Fsh).

 It is not possible to use Gemini and Google Assistant separately on Android. Once you agree to use Gemini, it will replace Assistant on your device. You can switch back to Assistant if you'd like, but then you won't be able to use the Gemini app.

##  How to Switch Back to Google Assistant From Gemini

 If you've tried Gemini and you're not ready to use it full-time, you can easily go back to using Google Assistant as your default digital assistant app.

 First, open the [Gemini app](https://www.anrdoezrs.net/links/3607085/type/dlg/sid/UUhtgUeUpU2002013/https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.bard) and tap your profile icon in the top corner. Select "Settings" from the menu.

![Gemini menu.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/screenshot_20240214-103410.png) 

 Scroll down and select "Digital Assistants from Google."

![Digital assistants from Google menu option.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/screenshot_20240214-103440.png)
