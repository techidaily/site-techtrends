## Wanderlust Dreamers' Guide: The 66 Premier Twitter Accounts for Amazing Deals, Sage Advice & Unforgettable Adventures

Close 

[ X (formerly Twitter)](https://www.lifewire.com/what-exactly-is-twitter-2483331) is a resource for many things—with travel advice, experiences, and opinions near the top of the list. In an ode to the legendary American cross-country[ Route 66](https://www.nps.gov/subjects/travelroute66/index.htm) , we gathered 66 of the most respected and consulted X feeds to help you plan (or fantasize) about your next journey.

 The traveling X accounts are categorized by:

* Guide and Service
* Writers, Photographers, and Bloggers
* Transportation Carriers

## Guide and Service 

![AAA Twitter](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/FZKPFncH3JkI9G5U69hPCOgOw68=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/AAATwitter-fe784e3ea07049c88da23efb0c57ff6e.jpg) 

[ @AAANews](https://twitter.com/aaanews) : The road tripper's best bet for help and discounts.

[ @Trip Advisor](https://twitter.com/TripAdvisor) : A tried-and-true full-service traveler's resource.

[ @LonelyPlanet:](https://twitter.com/lonelyplanet) The guide for off-beat and off-road exploration.

[ @SmarterTravel](https://twitter.com/smartertravel) : Boston-based clearinghouse for deals.

[ @LastMinute\_Com](https://twitter.com/lastminute%5Fcom) : Last-minute deals and alerts for a little wallet relief.

[ @CruiseLog](https://twitter.com/CruiseLog) :_USA Today_ 's Gene Sloan is your ferryman for leisure on the high seas.

[ @STI\_Travel](https://twitter.com/sti%5Ftravel) : Great nonprofit trying to leave the world a better place.

[ @TravelMagazine](https://twitter.com/TravelMagazine) : If it pertains to travel, you can find it here: news, articles, competitions, and more.

[ @FrugalTraveler](https://twitter.com/frugaltraveler) :_The New York Times'_ frugal traveler.

[ @FlightView](https://twitter.com/flightview) : Real-time flight information you can act on.

[ @Intrepid\_Travel](https://twitter.com/intrepid%5Ftravel) : The travel planner for adventurers who travel in packs. 

[ @BudgetTravel](https://twitter.com/budgettravel) : Making trips possible for the cash-strapped.

[ @KristinFinan](https://twitter.com/kristinfinan) :_Austin American-Statesman_ travel editor.

[ @airfarewatchdog](https://twitter.com/airfarewatchdog) : Low fare alerts, founded by @georgehobica and now a Trip Advisor brand.

[ @EuroCheapo](https://twitter.com/eurocheapo) : How to enjoy Europe without spending too much.

[ @TravelGov](https://twitter.com/TravelGov) : The official feed for advisories and more from the U.S. State Department.

[ @nytimestravel](https://twitter.com/nytimestravel) : Like a lot of sections from the_Times_ , one of the best resources available.

[ @JohnnyJet](https://twitter.com/JohnnyJet) : Seasoned sojourner sharing the deals he discovers.

[ @travel](https://twitter.com/travel) : A repository for all things world travel.

[ @CNTraveler](https://twitter.com/cntraveller) :_Condé Nast Traveler_ , a titan among travel magazines.

[ @FodorsTravel](https://twitter.com/fodorstravel) : A titan among travel guides.

[ @NatGeoTravel](https://twitter.com/NatGeoTravel) : The feed for the great_National Geographic Traveler_ .

[ @Expedia](https://twitter.com/Expedia) : Endless travel tips and breathtaking pictures.

[ @Australia](https://twitter.com/Australia) : All things Australia. Plus gorgeous pics.

[ @Kayak](https://twitter.com/KAYAK) : A trusted finder of travel deals.

[ @Gadling](https://twitter.com/gadling) : Living up to their claim to writing about fun, interesting, and relevant travel.

[ @MatadorNetwork](https://twitter.com/matadornetwork) : The biggest digital travel magazine with the best[ hashtag](https://www.lifewire.com/history-of-hashtags-3288940) : #TravelStoke.

[ @2Backpackers](https://twitter.com/2backpackers) : Where to go when you need travel guidance in Latin America.

[ @NWS](https://twitter.com/NWS) : The National Weather Service's feed, a reliable place for information on the most important factor in travel.

[ @BBC\_Travel](https://twitter.com/bbc%5Ftravel) : Neck and neck with_The New York Times_ as the finest international resource.

[ @statravelUS](https://twitter.com/statravelus) : An essential site for students and young people looking for discounts.

##  Writers, Photographers, and Bloggers 

![Twitter - Keith Jenkins ](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/83OuBjNTbq2qrd24oHmwcciNfN0=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/keithjenkinstwitter-f2b0f402e4b744e3a4382ed548572692.jpg)

[ @HeckticTravels](https://twitter.com/hecktictravels) Dalene and Pete Heck sold everything and hit the road.

[ @EverywhereTrip](https://twitter.com/everywheretrip) : X home of brilliant photographer Gary Arndt.

[ @Journeywoman](https://twitter.com/Journeywoman) : Canadian Evelyn Hannon is CEO of the largest online travel resource for women and a free tip newsletter, going to 70,000 women in 240 countries.

[ @LunaticAtLarge](https://twitter.com/lunaticatlarge) : Journalist Kristin Luna's funny and insightful feed.

[ @AdventureUncvrd](https://twitter.com/adventureuncvrd) : Influencing social and environmental change through responsible adventure.

[ @WildJunket](https://twitter.com/wildjunket) : Nellie Huang has been to more than 142 countries and all seven continents, but she's not a snob about it. Quite the contrary.

[ @holeinthedonut](https://twitter.com/holeinthedonut) : Barbara Weibel left corporate America to become a travel writer and photographer. So far, she's been to seven continents and over 100 countries.

[ @nomadicchick](https://twitter.com/nomadicchick) : Jeannie Mark was one of the early adopters of X as a travel destination.

[ @adventureblog02](https://twitter.com/adventureblog02) : Owner of The Adventure Blog, Kraig Becker shares photographs and his writings from all over the world.

[ @SeatGuru](https://twitter.com/seatguru) : A source for airplane seating, in-flight amenities, and airline information.

[ @melanie\_nayer](https://twitter.com/melanie%5Fnayer) : The feed for well-traveled thought leader Melanie Nayer.

[ @wendyperrin](https://twitter.com/wendyperrin) : Wendy Perrin left_Condé Nast Traveler_ to explore more of the world—and more travel media opportunities.

[ @Heather\_Poole](https://twitter.com/Heather%5FPoole) : Always good to make friends with the flight attendant. Poole gives you an insider's view on air travel.

[ @DreamofItaly](http://www.twitter.com/dreamofitaly) : If you dream of Italy, Kathy McCabe runs the[ Dream of Italy](http://www.dreamofitaly.com/) newsletter and uses X to post travel deals along with towns, restaurants, and hotels you might never know of otherwise.

[ @BlueBagNomads](https://twitter.com/lovetravelingto) : They share their housesitting adventures while traveling the world, including travel destinations, and travel tips.

[ @SamanthaBrown](https://twitter.com/samanthabrown) : Travel Channel host Samantha Brown's feed. Apparently, everyone has a crush on her: She once got a bizarre shout-out in a Bob Dylan song.

[ @bernabephoto](https://twitter.com/bernabephoto) : Globetrotting photographer and author, Richard Bernabe shares his visual inspiration from more than 60 countries.

[ @travelbloggersG](https://twitter.com/travelbloggersg) : Follow this group of Greeks and ex-pat travel bloggers as they explore the wonders of Greece.
