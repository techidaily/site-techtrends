##  How to See How Many Times an Instagram Post Has Been Saved 

 If you're less curious about who saved your post and instead want to see how many times it was saved, Instagram does let you do that. But it doesn't work from a personal account. First,[ switch your personal account to a creator account](https://www.lifewire.com/what-is-an-instagram-creator-account-4777670) , and then follow these steps to see your stats:

1. Tap your**profile** image to open your profile.
2. Choose the**post** you want to view the save count for.
3. Tap**View insights** . Several stats appear; the bookmark icon refers to how many times someone has saved this particular post.

 FAQ

* How can you see posts you've previously liked on Instagram?  
 To[ view posts you’ve previously liked](https://www.lifewire.com/see-previously-liked-photo-and-video-posts-4057657) , tap your**profile** icon in the app. Go to**menu** (three lines) >**Your activity** \>**Likes** . You can view the last 300 posts you’ve liked.
* How do you view archived posts on Instagram?  
 To[ view your archived Instagram posts](https://www.lifewire.com/archive-post-on-instagram-5271928) , tap your**profile** \>**menu** icon (three lines) >**Archive** .
* How do you repost an Instagram story?  
 To[ repost an Instagram story](https://www.lifewire.com/how-to-repost-instagram-story-4691376) , tap the**paper airplane** below the post, then select**Add to story** . For this to work, the other account must be public with post sharing or story sharing turned on.
