Technology advancements, like Artificial Intelligence (AI), are enabling state agencies to automate like never before. Challenges such as augmenting human review of incoming documents, handling the transition to REAL ID, and preventing fraud must be considered when deciding which technologies to utilize. This webinar recording will ensure that you are aware of the latest options to tackle your agency's document demand.  
  
During this webinar recording, we will cover:

* AI's role in augmenting document review in the government sector
* A preview of a best-in-class Proof-of-Identity portal
* Complimenting technologies and real-world experience
