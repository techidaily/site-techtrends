**What is AVI ?**  
[AVI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Audio%5FVideo%5FInterleave) is a multimedia container format introduced by Microsoft in November 1992 as part of its Video for Windows technology. It is compatible with many platforms and can contain both audio and video data in a file container that allows synchronous audio-with-video playback. Moreover, it can maintain the best possible quality compared with other video files, therefore is regarded as one of the most commonly used container formats.
