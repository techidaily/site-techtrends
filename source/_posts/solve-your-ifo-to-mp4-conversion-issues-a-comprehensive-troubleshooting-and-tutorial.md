## Part 1: Common Errors about IFO to MP4 Conversion 

_**Q: Why Error Messages Pop up during IFO to MP4 Conversion?**_

A: Almost all commercial DVD-video titles use [copy protection](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) method, intended to prevent against piracy, which also affects the IFO and VOB files. These measures are also called anti-ripping techs meant to foil general DVD ripper software, among which CSS is widely encoded on DVD discs. Consequently, many so-called free DVD ripper like Handbrake will malfunction with an error of "No valid source found", if CSS-decrypting program like libdvdcss, DVD Decrypter or so is uninstalled. Macrovision RipGuard, region codes, UOPs, Sony ARccOS and X-Project are also known for discouraging programs from copying the discs. So for a successful DVD IFO to MP4 ripping, a DVD decrypter or DVD ripper built in DVD decrypting feature is a must have to [rip protected DVD](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/). 

_**Q: The Conversion from IFO to MP4 Format Is Lengthy, Any Fast DVD Ripper Recommended?**_ 

A: You shall make it clear that slow process mainly depends on the max speed of DVD drive. Outdated computer CPU and RAM memory also slow down the ripping. On the flip side, the DVD ripper also has great impact on the converting speed. If the former two factors are foregone conclusions, then the program itself dominates. Feedbacks from users indicate an IFO to MP4 converter supporting hardware encoding technology or equipped with conversion speedup kernel is much more faster than ordinary DVD rippers. So it wise to take these decisive factors into consideration, if a [fast DVD ripper](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) matters. 

_**Q: Should I Convert IFO to MP4 H.264 or to MP4 MPEG4?**_

A. Many people are confused whether to [rip DVD to H.264](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) or convert DVD to MPEG4\. Both H.264 and MPEG4 are the encoding methods for MP4 video, they are widely accepted by browsers, devices, media players. But H.264 offers higher compression ratio, basically 1.5-2x more efficient than MPEG4 encoding. This make it feasible to record more information on the same hard disk, thus retaining 5-10% better quality and playback is more fluent than with the MPEG4 compression. So for high image quality, it's recommended to convert IFO to MP4 with H.264 codec. But if you want a smaller file size and faster IFO file to MP4 conversion, MPEG4 codec is preferred.

## Part 3: Basic Info about IFO and MP4 formats

[**What is IFO:**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VOB#Companion%5Ffiles) _IFO (information) files contain all the information a DVD player needs to know about a DVD. Startup-scenes, chapters, time to a movie stored on a DVD, audio tracks location, and subtitles information are included in the files._ 

[**What is MP4:**](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) _MPEG-4 Part 14, aka MP4, is a digital multimedia container format that stores video, audio, subtitles, still images, etc. MP4 is known for its high compression ratio, lower bandwidth consumption, and the widest compatibility with mobile phones, tablets, media players, tvs, game consoles, computers, etc and is the industry standard for a long time._
