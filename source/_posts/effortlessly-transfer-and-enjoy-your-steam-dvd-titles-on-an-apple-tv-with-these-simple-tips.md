### How to Stream DVD Movies to Apple TV via AirPlay Mirroring

But it doesn't mean there is no way to airplay DVD collections to Apple TV. A superior [DVD Player for Mac](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) with built-in Airplay function can do you favor. Let's see how to [Airplay DVD to Apple TV with 5KPlayer](https://tools.techidaily.com/5kplayer/products/) and VLC.

**Do It via 5KPlayer**

1. [Free download 5KPlayer](http://download.cnet.com/5KPlayer/3000-13632%5F4-76423535.html?part=dl-&subj=dl&tag=button) for your Mac/PCs and launch it.
2. Click Airplay on the up right corner of 5KPlayer interface and choose your Apple TV.
3. Drag and drop the DVD to 5KPlayer interface for playback. And you can see the DVD video displayed on your smart TV.

![Airplay DVD to Apple TV](https://www.macxdvd.com/mac-dvd-video-converter-how-to/article-image/5kp-airplay-to-apple-tv.jpg) 

**Tips:**

1. Connect your Apple TV and computer running Airplay to the same Wi-Fi network.
2. You should first [rip the DVD to MP4](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/), MOV or M4V and then use 5KPlayer to stream movies DVDs to Apple TV.

**Resort to VLC**

1. Go to "File > Open Disc…" in VLC and open your DVD.
2. Go to the top right hand side of your Mac desktop and click on the icon that looks like a TV to enable AirPlay Mirroring
3. Go to audio setting and click on Audio Device to open all the available options. Ensure that AirPlay is selected in this list.
4. Choose the video settings you would like to use to play the video with. The same steps as Audio setting.
5. Then, you see the VLC window on your TV. Double Click in the video window for fullscreen and start your movie.
