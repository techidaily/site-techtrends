## Part 1: Reasons that May Cause YouTube Not Working Problem

### 1\. Internet Connection

Despite the wide spread and faster speeds of the internet now being pushed through various technical advances, many users continue to suffer from the problems to quickly and consistently stream videos. In other words, if your internet connection is not good enough, you are very likely to get the **buffering problem**. Namely, even a low-quality video can lead to longtime buffering and choppy playback. For example, there is a YouTube error that it keeps saying no connection but it's not true since other websites are able to update indeed. To solve such YouTube is not working problems, you are suggested to shut down other bandwidth-consumed applications or resort to a proper [YouTube downloader](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) to save videos on Mac rather than streaming them online.   
**Also see:** [How to Solve YouTube Loading and Buffering Problem](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) 

### 2\. Issues About Browser

The ability of your browser or its installed extension can also affect YouTube and cause YouTube down problems. Sometimes it does not work if your web browser has too many files. To solve YouTube is not working problem like this, try to clear cookies and data from your temporary cache of your browser to make sure that your web page is empty. Or upgrade your browser and installed extensions to the latest version or shut down any conflicting plugins.  
There is another browser problem leading YouTube not working problem as well. That is YouTube no longer supports 4K video playback on Safari. Since it began encoding uploaded videos into VP9, Safari seems not allow 4K videos playback because it doesn't support VP9 codec at all. For this [YouTube error fixing](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/), you can switch to another browser like [enjoying YouTube 4K videos on Chrome](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) or fall back on a [Safari YouTube downloader](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) to save 4K videos offline.

### 3\. Security Problem

Your security system or firewall are designed to secure the internet and serves as a filter between your computer/network and the Internet. To put it another way, you may also get video not working and the like due to the blocking by your security system. For this error, check if YouTube is in the authorized websites in your security tool or firewall, if not, add it to the list or uninstall the blocking software. Then reload the YouTube video to see if the YouTube down problem has been resolved.
