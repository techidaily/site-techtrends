## Detailed Comparison on FLV and F4V

| **FLV**                   | **F4V**                                                                                                                                                               |                                                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Definition**            | **FLV** is short for Flash Video. It contains material encoded with codecs following the Sorenson Spark or VP 6 video compression formats.                            | **F4V** is a new type of FLV file. A F4V file is encoded based on the ISO base media file format (MPEG-4 PART 2) and is supported starting with Flash Player 9 update 3\. |
| **Description**           | Flash Video                                                                                                                                                           | Video for Adobe Flash Player                                                                                                                                               |
| **Supported media types** | **Video:** On2 VP6, Sorenson Spark (Sorenson H.263), Screen video, H.264 **Audio:** MP3, ADPCM, Linear PCM, Nellymoser, Speex, AAC, G.711 (reserved for internal use) | **Video:** H.264 Images (still frame of video data): GIF, PNG, JPEG **Audio:** AAC, HE-AAC, MP3                                                                            |
