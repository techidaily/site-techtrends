###  What to Know

* On Twitter.com, go to**More** \>**Settings and Privacy** \>**Change your password** .
* On the[ X (formerly Twitter) app](https://www.lifewire.com/best-mobile-twitter-apps-3486098) , go to**Settings and privacy** \>**Account** \>**Password** .
* To reset a forgotten password, select**Forgot password?** on the sign-in page.

 You should update your X (formerly Twitter) credentials often to keep your account secure and safe from hackers. Here are several ways to change an X password.

##  How to Change Your X Password from Twitter.com

 If you know your password and want to change it, sign in to your[ X account](https://twitter.com/) to make the change.

1. Sign in to your X account using your current password.
2. Select**More** in the left vertical panel.  
![Twitter - select More](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/1fGnwRSAkplJdGjxbsNeKUiDQEs=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ann1newSelectMore-3d9696ba30344033be6bcd68a81d948d.jpg)
3. Select**Settings and privacy** .  
![Twitter - select Settings and privacy](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/I5KE3PMYtws7OvvQ3c7x0LbyoBE=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ann2SelectSettingsandprivacyleftpanel-3fb6f15cc7d54bf09a55ada4a9b95994.jpg)
4. Under the**Your Account** heading, select**Change your password** .  
![Twitter - Your Account - Change your password](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/q4u6K0gWNxE0G8wP6PHxc18J_Bw=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ann3SelectChangeyourpassword-7ae9e062759c45c79a2f046ca3d36093.jpg)
5. Enter your current password in the**Current password** text box.
6. Enter the new password you want to use in the**New password** text box.
7. Enter the new password a second time in the**Confirm password** text box.  
![Twitter - enter current and new password](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/WIXDT21BZhc1_Rhaq-Mh3gvIFFo=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ann4-entercurrentandnewpasswordinformation-0f2c527135c74fc0bd9b7843f88ae102.jpg)
8. Select**Save** when you’re done.
9. You may be asked to review applications that can access your account. Select**Review applications** to see the apps that require your new log in credentials.

 To add even more security to your X account, set up[ two-factor authentication](https://www.lifewire.com/how-to-use-two-factor-authentication-4686242) . Two-factor authentication means that you'll use your X password to log in—plus one of the following: a security code, confirmation from another app, or a text message.

1. Open your favorite mobile web browser.

3. Sign in using your current password.
4. Tap your**profile image** in the upper-left corner.
5. Tap**Settings and privacy** .
6. Tap**Your account** .

##  Reset an X Password You've Lost or Forgotten

 Passwords get lost and forgotten. When you can’t remember your X password, reset it.

 X requires an email address or a phone number to reset a lost or forgotten password. Make sure you’ve added one or both to your account.

1. Go to the X sign-in page.
2. Select**Forgot password?**  
![Twitter - forgot password](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/3bE_c9uJ65rKOiacJdoI_8Nb0ho=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ann1forgotpassword-cb611a9a41df489f8e1c6db5f254cfc1.jpg)
3. Enter your email address, phone number, or X username in the text box.

4. Select**Search** . You’ll be asked to choose how to reset your password.

6. Select**Continue** . A message appears telling you to open your email or check your text messages.
7. Open the email or text message, then select**Reset password** .
8. Enter your new password in the**Type your new password** text box.
9. Enter your new password a second time in the**Type your new password one more** **time** text box.  
![Twitter - enter new password](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/gxd7mbf9m3mCS3I4EJXcbff422A=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ann4Typeinnewpassword-a8553fd019d24394b7dc63720706b4e7.jpg)

11. Sign in to X with your username and new password.
