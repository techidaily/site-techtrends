How to Use AirPods With an HP Laptop

Connecting AirPods to an HP laptop isn’t the same as actually using the AirPods with the laptop. If you want to use your AirPods to listen to music or videos, or video chat, or anything else, you’ll need to switch the audio output to be your earbuds. This may happen automatically the first time your AirPods connect, but you can also do it manually if you find that audio isn’t coming through.

Your laptop may have multiple audio options if you’ve connected a variety of devices in the past, but an HP laptop will show Speaker / Headphone (Realtek(R) Audio) at a minimum. Switching from that output to your AirPods will let you use your AirPods with your laptop.

Do AirPods Work With Laptops?

AirPods work with any device designed to connect to an audio output or input device via Bluetooth. This means most laptops will work, especially brand-new ones. Since all modern HP laptops are equipped with Bluetooth, you can use AirPods with an HP laptop the same way you can use any other Bluetooth earbuds, headphones, or headset.

You canadd a Bluetooth adapter to your laptop if you need to.

Why Can’t I Connect My AirPods to My HP Laptop?

If your AirPods won’t connect even after following the steps above, it could be an issue with Bluetooth on your laptop or a problem with your Airpods. Here are some common problems and solutions:

  • Fix Bluetooth problems . There could be an issue with your device’s Bluetooth. This is especially true if none of your other Bluetooth devices seem to work with your laptop.
  • Update your PC’s drivers . If your Bluetooth driver is outdated or missing, you’ll have problems connecting to your earbuds.
  • Put the AirPods into pairing mode . The white light on your AirPods case needs to be flashing or you won’t be able to make the initial connection.

  • How do I connect my AirPods to a Mac?
    To connect your AirPods directly to your Mac, make sure your AirPods are in their case, then open the lid. Press and hold thesetup button until you see it flash white. On your Mac, go toSystem Preferences >Bluetooth > in theDevices list, selectAirPods .
  • How do I connect AirPods to a Chromebook?
    Toconnect AirPods to a Chromebook , selectMenu on the Chromebook, then selectBluetooth and turn on the Bluetooth connection. Press and hold thesetup button on the AirPods case, then, on the Chromebook, go to the Bluetooth Available Devices list and select theAirPods .
  • How do I connect AirPods to an Android device?
    Toconnect AirPods to an Android device , openSettings on the Android and toggle on Bluetooth. Press and hold thesetup button on the AirPods case until the light flashes white, then, on the Android device, tap Airpods from the available devices list.
  • How do I connect AirPods to a Roku TV?
    Touse AirPods with a Roku TV , pair your AirPods with an iPhone or Android, then download and install the Roku app. In the Roku app, selectRemote >Devices >OK >Connect Now . Select your Roku device, then tap theRemote icon >headphones icon>OK .
