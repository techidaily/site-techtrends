How to Sign Up for Google Gemini

You can sign up on the web with your Google account or use the Android mobile app. Both methods are straightforward and offer a clean interface for interacting with Gemini.

Using the Web App

Go to the dedicatedGoogle Gemini homepage and use your Google personal account to sign in. You can also create a new Google account if you don’t have one yet (or don’t want your primary account tied to Gemini).

The web app is supported in most browsers, such as Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Opera, or Edge. Gemini supports 40 languages and is available in over 230 countries.

You don’t have to accept Gemini’s original answer. The generative AI chatbot offers a few extra methods to change the style of the answer.

  2. Give Gemini’s large language model some feedback by choosing theGood response orBad response icons (the thumb icons).
  3. TheModify response filter pop-up helps you adjust the length, simplify the language, and change the tone of a response. Use this to tailor the answers for your intended audience or finetune them.
  4. Instead of making Gemini rewrite the entire content, you can modify parts of the generated responses. Highlight any part of the text and select theModify selected text icon.

Using Extensions in Gemini

With Gemini’s extensions, you can tap into Google’s ecosystem of apps to cover a wider variety of everyday uses. Most extensions are on by default, but you may need to give Gemini permission to use them in your prompt.

  1. Go toSettings >Extensions .
  2. Enable or disable your choice of extensions with the toggle switch.
  3. To automatically use any Google services as an extension, mention it in the prompt. Alternatively, enter @ and select the extension to allow Gemini to use a specific extension.

More Tips for Using Gemini Effectively

Here are a few more tips to make better use of Gemini.

  • To use Gemini in Dark mode, go toSettings >Dark theme .
  • Choose an item in the recent chats list on the sidebar, and then select the three vertical dots and choosePin orRename to keep track of important threads.
  • Use concise prompts and carry a multi-step conversation to help Gemini understand the logic of your question.
  • You can prompt Gemini to make a table. Then, chooseExport to Sheets at the foot of the table to send it to Google Sheets.
  • You can paste directly into the prompt field–for instance, from another Chatbot or app–or use the microphone to dictate the prompt.
